Welcome to SRGI – Shaping Futures, Building Careers

SR Group of Institutions (SRGI) is a leading educational hub in northern India, renowned for its commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and holistic student development. Established in 2002, SRGI offers a vibrant campus, state-of-the-art facilities, and a diverse range of technical and professional courses designed to empower students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Why Choose SRGI?

State of the Art Infrastructure: 80-acre lush green campus equipped with advanced classrooms, labs, workshops, sports fields, Wi-Fi connectivity and modern amenities.

Diverse Community: Students from across India and beyond, fostering a cosmopolitan environment.

Industry-Aligned Curriculum: Strong emphasis on research, project work, and industry collaboration.

Placement Excellence: Collaborations with reputed organisations, strong alumni record and excellent placement packages.

From Chairman's Desk

"Education is not just the acquisition of knowledge, but the awakening of the mind, character, and purpose."

SR Group of Institutions began its inspiring journey in the year 2002 with a clear mission — to disseminate quality education and foster meaningful research across the fields of Science, Engineering, Agriculture, Pharmacy, Legal Studies, Management, and Humanities.

The very foundation of SRGI was laid with the vision of cultivating enthusiasm for diverse knowledge while instilling strong social, intellectual, and professional values in a warm, disciplined, and homely academic environment. We aspire to serve as a role model by providing mentorship that ignites young minds, strengthens leadership qualities, and builds self-confidence to face scientific and technological challenges for the betterment of the nation.

Our focus is not merely on learning, but on awakening the power of critical thinking and encouraging students to rise to higher levels of meta-cognition and innovation. Through fully structured, job-oriented courses, strong industry collaborations, modern infrastructure, and highly dedicated faculty members from reputed academic institutions, we strive to shape responsible professionals and future leaders.

Our technical and supporting staff work tirelessly behind the scenes to ensure a smooth, efficient, and student-centric academic environment. In today’s rapidly evolving academic ecosystem, teaching, learning, and research demand constant reorientation. We remain committed to embracing these changes by dedicating greater resources and continuously reforming our educational approaches.

SRGI has proudly positioned itself among the leading centers of higher education by ensuring transparency, progressive administration, and quality-driven governance. To meet the emerging dynamics of the education system, we emphasize capacity building through innovative blends of pedagogy and andragogy, supported by modern technology and industry–academia collaboration.

Our sprawling, lush green campus stands as a symbol of harmony between nature and knowledge, fostering holistic development, discipline, confidence, and creativity among students and faculty alike.

On behalf of the SR Group of Institutions, I extend my heartfelt best wishes to all learners as they embark on this enriching journey of knowledge, wisdom, and transformation.

Surendra Kumar Rai
Chairman
